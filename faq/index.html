Frequently asked questions

About my size...

You can click here to learn how to properly measure your nails. If you need any additional help, (it's okay, I got you) shoot me an email to request a Sample Sizing Pack to ensure they fit perfectly and then just email your sizes.

How long do they last?

Depending on nail prep and type of glue used, press on nails can last between 1-3 weeks

When Will My Order Ship?

All sets are custom made. Current processing time to create nails is 1 week.. Orders may be completed sooner, based on orders and work flow. Orders placed on the weekend and/or select holidays will be processed the next business day.

What comes with my nails?

All nail orders come with your custom press on nails, nail file, orange wood stick (cuticle pusher), mini buffer, glue, alcohol prep pad and application and removal instructions. If you would like to order adhesive tabs, we have those for purchase as well. We suggest trying both and seeing which method works best for you.

What if the nails I purchased don't fit?

If the wrong size is ordered we are not held responsible. The sample sizing pack is for those that are unsure of their correct size along with our sizing guide that can be found here to measure for the proper fit. If the size is off on a nail or two, we suggest just filing it with the file in the kit we provide with each purchase

How durable are the press ons?

These press ons are very durable! (and personally tested) Each set is cured with 4+ coats of gel that adds to the strong & high-quality tip itself.

Custom or change of design?

Of course! All sets are made to order and can be customized. If you prefer a different color, matte or glossy finish, shorter length or small design changes, simply make a note in the special requests. For bigger changes that include adding on more or changing crystals please contact us first to approve changes or invoice you for a custom order.

Do you accept cancellations, returns or exchanges?

We gladly accept cancellations within 24 hours of purchase.

After the 24 hours ALL SALES ARE FINAL which means you fully agree to wait on the time it takes to create your order. No Refunds will be processed after the 24 hour purchase.

Also due to the nature of our custom handmade products we do not accept returns or make any exchanges (except in the case of products delivered in defective or damaged state. If so, email nailsbytam.h@gmail.com and include an image of the defected or damaged product).
